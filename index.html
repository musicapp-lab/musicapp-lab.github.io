Quick guide What the practice buttons do
PlayPlay / pauseStarts the backing sound and listens for your pitch when scoring is available.
Start OverReset positionReturns the cursor to the beginning of the piece or selected section.
RecordSave a takeArms audio recording for the next playback so you can review it later.
TempoSpeedUse -/+ buttons or the slider to slow down or speed up playback.
MetronomePulse supportAdds a click so entries and releases can be checked against a steady beat.
ScoringScoringTurns mic scoring on. If 7-minute access is inactive, a reward ad is requested when you turn it on.
PlaybackReference soundTurns the guide sound on or off while keeping the score visible.
In BbNotation keyShows the selected part notation and lets you nudge display transposition by semitone.
FocusClean score viewHides extra panels and gives the score more room on small screens.
ScrollScore viewScroll, page, and performance views change how much of the score stays on screen.
SectionLoop measuresSet start/end measures and repeat only the phrase you want to fix.
8vaDisplay octaveMoves the notation octave for easier reading without changing your target part.
+ / -Score sizeZooms the score in or out without changing the pitch target.
FeedbackLive feedbackThe meter and note labels show pitch center, scoring, and confidence while you play.
DrawScore marksUse draw, color, and clear for temporary practice marks on the score.
PartsMulti-part scoresChoose which part is scored and which parts remain as accompaniment when a score has multiple parts.
